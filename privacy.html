Privacy Policy

How the QuickBooks MCP Connector collects, uses, stores, and protects information for internal operational use.

Effective date: March 8, 2026
Product: QuickBooks MCP Connector
Purpose: Internal business integration with QuickBooks Online and related tooling

1. Scope

This policy applies to the QuickBooks MCP Connector and related internal workflows used to access and process QuickBooks Online data for legitimate business operations.

2. Information We Access

Depending on enabled permissions and integration use cases, the connector may access business information such as chart of accounts, vendors, customers, invoices, transactions, and related accounting metadata.

3. How Information Is Used

  • To authenticate and maintain authorized QuickBooks connections.
  • To retrieve or synchronize accounting data requested by internal workflows.
  • To support operational reporting, bookkeeping, or automation tasks.
  • To monitor, troubleshoot, and secure the integration.

4. Legal and Operational Basis

Information is processed for legitimate internal business purposes and only within the scope required to operate the integration and support authorized accounting workflows.

5. Sharing

Information accessed by the connector is not sold. Data may be shared only with authorized personnel, approved service providers, or platform operators as necessary to run the integration, comply with legal obligations, or protect systems and records.

6. Storage and Security

Reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures are used to protect credentials, tokens, and accessed data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

7. Retention

Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to support operational, accounting, audit, security, or legal requirements, after which it may be deleted or anonymized where appropriate.

8. Third-Party Platforms

The connector relies on third-party infrastructure and APIs, including Intuit QuickBooks Online. Those providers maintain their own privacy and security practices, which also apply to their services.

9. Your Choices

Access to the connector is controlled internally. Requests regarding access, correction, deletion, or restriction of data should be directed to the internal system administrator or business owner responsible for the integration.

10. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect operational, legal, or security changes. The latest version will be posted at this URL with the updated effective date.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact your internal administrator or designated business owner for this integration.
